About Keith Musig

I am originally from Salt Lake City, Utah and graduated from the University of Utah with a Bachelor of Fine Arts Degree in Illustration. I have worked as a marketing coordinator, graphic designer and web designer throughout my career. I have played guitar for many years. I once studied guitar from Robert Fripp. I have played jazz guitar since college and perform semi regularly at McMenamins Spanish Ballroom in Tacoma.
